Washry

Aplikasi IOS Pencucian Mobil & Motor

This is an online booking app designed for local auto workshop who wish to promote their shop digitally, making it easier for them to be discovered by potential customers. Our application authenticates customers' orders with QR technology, guaranteeing its validity. There are two client-side applications for our product, that is, the client for customers and one different client for shop owners. Users can find local workshops using maps technology from using the customer application, then choose their preferred services. Our merchant application is designed for shop owner to manage their available services and orders.

  • Klien: Washry
  • Kategori: Aplikasi
